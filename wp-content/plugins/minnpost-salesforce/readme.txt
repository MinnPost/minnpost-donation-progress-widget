== Installation ==

1. Upload the `minnpost-salesforce` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Fill out the settings on the MinnPost subpage of the Salesforce subpage, under the Settings menu
